The recipe of Enchilladas

The enchilladas is like taco but the tortilla is folded on the plate.
You put cheese on the top and a lot of salsa from green tomato and chili.

  • Corn flour
  • Chili
  • Chicken breast
  • Lemon
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Green tomate
  1. Mix the corn flour with enough water.
  2. Cook the chicken breast in water with garlix and onion.
  3. Pull the meat in small pieces
  4. Triturate the salsa of the chiles, garlic, onion, lemon and green tomate.
  5. Put some salsa inside of the torilla put the meat and roll the torilla.
  6. Put cheese and salsa on the top of the tortilla
  7. Enjoy!
